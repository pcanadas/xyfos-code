Xyfos Sales is the first product under Xyfos Code. It’s an offer-centric CRM for sales teams. It helps sales teams manage their offers, track their sales pipeline, and close more deals. It’s built with AI and machine learning to help sales teams be more productive and close more deals. It’s currently in alpha and we’re looking for early adopters to try it out and give us feedback. If you’re interested, please [sign up here](https://app.xyfos.com).
